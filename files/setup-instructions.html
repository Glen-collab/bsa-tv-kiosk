Setting up your Gym TV

Grab your phone — takes about 60 seconds.

  1. Open the WiFi settings on your phone.
  2. Connect to the network called BSA-Kiosk-Setup.
  3. A setup page opens automatically — pick your gym's WiFi and enter the password.
  4. This screen will change to your workouts within 30 seconds.
